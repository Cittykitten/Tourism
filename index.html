Tamale, the vibrant capital of the Northern Region of Ghana,
is renowned for its rich culture and warm hospitality.
Visitors can explore the bustling central market,
the mesmerizing mud-brick mosque,
and the nearby exciting Mole National Park for wildlife spotting.

Mognori Eco-Village



Mognori Eco-Village in the Northern Region of Ghana is a sustainable community focused on eco-friendly living and agriculture.
It aims to promote environmental conservation while providing a space for cultural exchange and education.
The village often features organic farming practices, renewable energy sources, and community-driven initiatives.
Visitors can experience local traditions, participate in workshops, and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
It's a great place to learn about sustainability and immerse yourself in the local culture.
